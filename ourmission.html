What we do?

Our mission is to save dogs from cruelty and save people from hungry dogs attacks. Every year hundreds of people are attacked from dogs, and even more dogs are killed by people.
We want to save both of them by giving dogs a place to stay and food to eat. We can only do this with the help of our partners, municipality and restaurants of the city. Municipality's role is to select the house suitable for dogs and to pay the workers while restaurants will participate by donating their food remains.It is worth mentioning that by this cooperation of municipality and restaurants thousands euro will be saved.
